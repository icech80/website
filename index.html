BienvenidosWelcome

Somos una comunidad cristiana ubicada en Calle Echaurren #80, Santiago. Nos reunimos para estudiar la Palabra de Dios, orar juntos y edificarnos mutuamente en la fe. Le invitamos a conocernos y ser parte de nuestra familia.We are a Christian community located at Calle Echaurren #80, Santiago. We gather to study the Word of God, pray together, and build each other up in faith. We invite you to get to know us and become part of our family.
